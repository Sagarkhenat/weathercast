Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Weather Track collects location data to provide accurate local weather forecasts. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

Data Usage
  • Location: To fetch weather for your current coordinates.
  • Storage: To save your favorite cities for offline viewing.
  • Device Info: To optimize the display for your specific screen size.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Capacitor plugins to access device hardware. Weather data is fetched via reputable APIs (like OpenWeatherMap), which may receive your coordinates to return the correct forecast.

3. Security

We prioritize your data security. Since Weather Cast is a client-side application built with Angular, your personal settings remain within the app's local sandbox.
